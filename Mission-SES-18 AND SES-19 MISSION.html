MARCH 17, 2023

SES-18 AND SES-19 MISSION

On Friday, March 17 at 7:38 p.m. ET, Falcon 9 launched SES-18
and SES-19 to a geosynchronous transfer orbit from Space Launch
Complex 40 (SLC-40) at Cape Canaveral Space Force Station in Florida

This was the sixth launch and landing of this booster,
which previously supported the launch CRS-24,
Eutelsat HOTBIRD 13F,OneWeb 1, and two Starlink missions.
