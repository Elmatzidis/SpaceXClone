MARCH 24, 2023

STARLINK MISSION

On Friday, March 24 at 11:33 a.m. ET, Falcon 9 launched 56
Starlink satellites to low-Earth orbit from Space Launch Complex
40 at Cape Canaveral Space Force Station, Florida

This was the tenth launch and landing for this Falcon 9 first stage
booster, which previously launched CRS-22, Crew-3, Turksat 5B, Crew-4,
CRS-25, Eutelsat HOTBIRD 13G, mPOWER-a and now three Starlink missions.
